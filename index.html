Course Overview

This self-paced online course is designed to empower Keller ISD middle school educators to develop essential digital literacy skills and effectively integrate educational technology into classroom instruction. Based on the Holistic 4D Model, the course supports real-world application and ongoing digital growth.

Target Audience: Middle School Teachers in Keller ISD

Project URL: https://yourprojectlink.com/IDProjectPart2

Course Modules

  • Fundamentals of Digital Literacy
  • Incorporating Technology in Daily Instruction
  • Effective Use of LMS Tools
  • Digital Citizenship and Online Safety

Implementation Plan

  • Pilot Rollout: Launch with a small test group of teachers to gather feedback and address usability issues.
  • LMS Integration: Course hosted on the district’s Canvas LMS platform with full functionality.
  • Orientation: Zoom session introducing the course features, navigation, and support.
  • Support: Email helpdesk and a peer support forum throughout course access.
  • Analytics Monitoring: Weekly LMS usage reviews and progress tracking.

Summative Evaluation Plan

We use Kirkpatrick’s Four Levels of Evaluation to assess course effectiveness:

  • Reaction: Participant feedback through post-course surveys.
  • Learning: Pre- and post-assessments to measure knowledge gains.
  • Behavior: Follow-up reflections and leadership feedback on classroom integration.
  • Results: Observed changes in teaching practices and tech adoption in classrooms.

Evaluation data will guide future revisions and district-level scalability.
